FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How quickly can an AI agent be live for my business?
Most AI voice agents and receptionists go live within 2-4 weeks of our discovery call, depending on the complexity of your workflows and integrations.
Will the AI sound robotic or unnatural?
No. We use advanced voice models tuned for natural pacing, tone, and conversational flow — most callers can't tell they're speaking with an AI.
Can the AI integrate with our existing tools (CRM, calendar, etc.)?
Yes. We integrate with most popular CRMs, calendars, helpdesks, and messaging platforms including HubSpot, Calendly, Google Calendar, WhatsApp, and more.
What does pricing look like?
Pricing depends on the scope of automation and number of agents needed. We offer transparent monthly plans with no long-term lock-in — book a call for a custom quote.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Absolutely. Every plan includes ongoing monitoring, optimization, and support so your AI agents keep improving as your business grows.
